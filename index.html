We welcome all to join our crew whether you play the game for the characters, story, grids, or even the grind! Our goal is just to enjoy Granblue with others, which is why our crew is open to members of all ranks. We know how easy it is to fall off this game once the MMO grind shows itself, so we're here to ease any newcomers into the game and make it as enjoyable of a time as possible.

  • You're new to the game and need some help from veterans but don't feel like constantly asking on the weekly threads.

  • You love theory crafting and constantly have the wiki open
  • You just like playing the game but don't feel like committing days of your life to Guild Wars (We still do Guild Wars just not as extreme as other crews)
